Membership Application

Thank you for your interest in joining the Regen Commons. As we begin to open membership, we're onboarding the initial participants who contributed to the Regen Commons governance design process, alongside a close-knit cohort of Steward-attested peers to seed the commons and initialize self-governance. Together, you will make up the first cohort of Regen Commons Members.

If you're not part of one of those two groups, you're still able to apply, but your application will not be considered until we open the second cohort.

However you ended up here, your responses to the following questions will help the steward council understand who you are, what you care about, and how you might contribute to our shared commons.

There are no trick questions. We're looking for honesty, alignment, and genuine interest — not perfect answers. Please write in your own voice without over-reliance on generative AI.

Your full responses will be shared with the steward council. Basic details (name, role, organization) will be displayed publicly on the Regen Commons website. Some contact details will be shared with other Commons Members.

What happens next

Thank you for submitting.

The council will review your responses, and your candidacy will enter a review period during which existing members may offer input. You'll hear back from your nominating steward within the cohort's review cycle.

If you have questions at any point, reach out to the person who invited you — they're your primary point of contact through this process.

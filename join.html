Step 4 of 4

Your commitment

Membership is a shared commitment. These are the principles that hold the commons together. Read them. If they ring true, this is your place.

Care We tend to the commons and to each other. Care is the operational foundation of any durable collective.
Stewardship We hold the commons in trust for those who come after us. Not ownership, not control.
Transparency We iterate in public. We name our developmental stage honestly. We don't present aspiration as fact.
Reciprocity Benefits flow back to those who nurture the commons. We guard against extraction — from communities, from labour, from knowledge.
Plurality We honour many expressions of regenerative practice while sustaining coherence. The commons is a container for multiple definitions.
Collaboration We build with, not for. No voice dominates the identity.
Integrity We are what we say we are. Constitution, brand, and lived experience must be aligned.
